Hannah Jeong is a designer, artist and night owl from the east coast, pursuing a BFA in Graphic Design at RISD.

She is most excited about using design as a means of connecting people, with empathy and play at the core of her creative approach.

Enjoys staring at the sky, matcha lattes, (walk)running, learning guitar, and listening to 90’s rnb.
