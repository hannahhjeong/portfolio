The War in Ukraine Drawn by Children

This web reader is based on Brian McCarty's five-week expedition in Ukraine with non-profit organization WarToys, an organization whcih helps create safe spaces for children impacted by the traumas of war to share their stories through art therapy initiatives.

The children's narratives and drawings are brought to life interactively, offering readers a profound understanding and immersion into their stories.
